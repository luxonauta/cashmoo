Welcome to CashMoo!

Get started with your financial journey by following these simple steps. Each step will help you build a complete picture of your finances.

  1. Add your first income

    Start by recording your salary, freelance payments, or any other money you receive regularly. This helps track your total earning potential.

  2. Add your first expense

    Record your monthly bills, rent, groceries, or any recurring costs. This shows where your money goes each month.

  3. Add your credit card

    Register your credit cards with their limits and payment dates. This helps monitor your credit usage and avoid overspending.

  4. Set a financial goal

    Define what you want to achieve financially, like saving for vacation or building an emergency fund. Goals keep you motivated!

  5. Enable notifications

    Turn on reminders for bill due dates and goal milestones. Never miss a payment or lose track of your progress again.

Tip: Start with just one item in each category. You can always add more details later as you get comfortable with the app.
