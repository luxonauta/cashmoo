How Notifications Work

  • Local notifications: All alerts are generated directly by CashMoo on your computer - no internet connection required.
  • Privacy first: Your financial data never leaves your device. Notifications are created using your local data only.
  • Bill reminders: Automatic alerts help you avoid late fees and maintain good payment history, even when you're offline.
  • Goal tracking: Progress updates keep you motivated toward your financial objectives.
